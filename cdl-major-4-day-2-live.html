CDL Major IV Day 2 Live: Scores, Results, and Match Updates

Follow our live coverage from Paris as CDL Major IV Day 2 unfolds with elimination matches, winners bracket clashes, live scores, map results, and every major moment throughout the tournament.

June 27, 2026 by Respawnerz Staff Live, updating

Day two of CDL Major IV is underway in Paris, and the opening series has taken another dramatic turn. Miami Heretics struck first with a convincing Gridlock Hardpoint victory before Paris Gentle Mates answered back in Fringe Search and Destroy to level the series. The momentum didn't last long, however, as Heretics reclaimed control with a dominant 4-1 win on Gridlock Overload to move within a single map of advancing. With Scar Hardpoint up next, Gentle Mates now face a must-win situation to keep their tournament hopes alive.

Day 2 raises the stakes even further at CDL Major IV. With the opening round now complete, the field has begun to narrow as teams battle to stay in the winners bracket or avoid elimination altogether. Hosted by Gentle Mates in Paris, the tournament features all 12 CDL franchises competing for a share of the $365,000 prize pool, including $150,000 and 100 CDL Points for the eventual champions. Every match remains a best-of-five until the Grand Final, which expands to a best-of-seven, meaning every map carries extra weight as teams fight to build momentum heading toward Call of Duty Champs in Las Vegas.

Miami Heretics lead Paris Gentle Mates 2-1

The opening elimination series of Day 2 has swung back in Miami Heretics' favor after three maps. Heretics opened the match with a comfortable 250-167 victory on Gridlock Hardpoint, using superior hill rotations and objective control to establish an early lead before Paris Gentle Mates responded with an impressive 6-2 win on Fringe Search and Destroy.

With the series tied, Gridlock Overload proved to be the turning point. Miami Heretics controlled the objective from start to finish, securing a convincing 4-1 victory to move ahead 2-1 in the best-of-five. The result leaves Paris Gentle Mates with no room for error as they head into Scar Hardpoint needing consecutive map wins to avoid elimination on home soil.

Scar Hardpoint is now set to decide whether Heretics can close out the series or if Gentle Mates can force a decisive fifth map on Hacienda Search and Destroy. Respawnerz will continue updating this page live as CDL Major IV Day 2 unfolds in Paris.

Miami Heretics are now one map away from advancing, while Paris Gentle Mates need back-to-back victories to keep their Major IV campaign alive. With more elimination matches still to come throughout the day, stay with Respawnerz for live scores, series results, standout performances, and every major moment from CDL Major IV in Paris.
