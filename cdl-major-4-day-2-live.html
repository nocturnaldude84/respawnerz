CDL Major IV Live: Miami Heretics Take Map 1, Paris Gentle Mates Respond to Level Series 1-1

Day 2 of CDL Major IV is underway in Paris. Miami Heretics opened the series with a convincing Hardpoint victory before Paris Gentle Mates hit back in Search and Destroy to even the score.

June 27, 2026 by Respawnerz Staff Live, updating

Day two of CDL Major IV is underway in Paris, and the first series has already delivered an early momentum swing. Miami Heretics drew first blood with a comfortable victory on Gridlock Hardpoint, but the hometown favorites refused to let the series slip away. Paris Gentle Mates answered emphatically on Fringe Search and Destroy, taking the map 6-2 to level the series at one map apiece. With Gridlock Overload up next, both teams remain just two map wins away from advancing as Respawnerz continues live coverage throughout the day.

Day 2 raises the stakes even further at CDL Major IV. With the opening round now complete, the field has begun to narrow as teams battle to stay in the winners bracket or avoid elimination altogether. Hosted by Gentle Mates in Paris, the tournament features all 12 CDL franchises competing for a share of the $365,000 prize pool, including $150,000 and 100 CDL Points for the eventual champions. Every match remains a best-of-five until the Grand Final, which expands to a best-of-seven, meaning every map carries extra weight as teams fight to build momentum heading toward Call of Duty Champs in Las Vegas.

Paris Gentle Mates vs. Miami Heretics (Tied 1-1)

The opening winners bracket series of Day 2 has quickly turned into a best-of-three after both teams traded dominant map victories. Miami Heretics looked in complete control to start proceedings, cruising to a 250-167 win on Gridlock Hardpoint. Their coordinated rotations and hill control kept Gentle Mates chasing the score for much of the opening map, allowing Heretics to take an early 1-0 lead in the series.

Paris Gentle Mates responded immediately on Fringe Search and Destroy. After dropping the Hardpoint, the hosts looked far more composed in the tactical game mode, shutting down Miami's attacks and claiming a convincing 6-2 victory to level the series. The response keeps the Paris crowd firmly behind Gentle Mates heading into the pivotal third map.

With the game now tied 1-1, attention shifts to Gridlock Overload, a map that could prove decisive in determining who takes control of the winners bracket matchup. Respawnerz will continue updating this page with live scores, map results, and key moments as the action unfolds.

Day 2 is only just getting started, and with every series carrying major implications for the winners bracket and elimination rounds, there's plenty more Call of Duty still to come in Paris. Stay with Respawnerz as we continue updating this live blog with map scores, series results, standout performances, and every major moment from CDL Major IV throughout the day.
