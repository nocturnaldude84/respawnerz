CDL Major IV Day 2 Live: Scores, Results, and Match Updates

Follow our live coverage from Paris as CDL Major IV Day 2 unfolds with elimination matches, winners bracket clashes, live scores, map results, and every major moment throughout the tournament.

June 27, 2026 by Respawnerz Staff Live, updating

Day two of CDL Major IV is underway in Paris, and the opening series has taken another dramatic turn. Miami Heretics struck first with a convincing Gridlock Hardpoint victory before Paris Gentle Mates answered back in Fringe Search and Destroy to level the series. The momentum didn't last long, however, as Heretics reclaimed control with a dominant 4-1 win on Gridlock Overload to move within a single map of advancing. With Scar Hardpoint up next, Gentle Mates now face a must-win situation to keep their tournament hopes alive.

Hosted by Gentle Mates in Paris, the tournament features all 12 CDL franchises competing for a share of the $365,000 prize pool, including $150,000 and 100 CDL Points for the eventual champions. Every match remains a best-of-five until the Grand Final, which expands to a best-of-seven, meaning every map carries extra weight as teams fight to build momentum heading toward Call of Duty Champs in Las Vegas.

Paris Gentle Mates def. Miami Heretics 3-2

The opening elimination series of CDL Major IV Day 2 delivered the first reverse comeback of the day, with Paris Gentle Mates recovering from the brink of elimination to defeat Miami Heretics 3-2 and keep their Major campaign alive on home soil.

Miami Heretics struck first with a commanding 250-167 victory on Gridlock Hardpoint before Gentle Mates answered with a dominant 6-2 win on Fringe Search and Destroy. Heretics then moved within a single map of advancing after claiming Gridlock Overload 4-1, leaving the hosts needing back-to-back victories to survive.

Paris refused to fold. They edged out Miami Heretics 250-227 on Scar Hardpoint to force a deciding fifth map before producing their most convincing performance of the series on Hacienda Search and Destroy. Gentle Mates completed the comeback with a flawless 6-0 sweep, eliminating Miami Heretics and booking their place in the next round of Major IV. Ghosty was instrumental throughout the series, earning the Monster Energy Winners Spotlight after finishing with 84 kills, a 1.04 K/D, 10,792 damage, and a 1.60 Search and Destroy K/D.

It was a remarkable turnaround after trailing 2-1, with the French crowd roaring Gentle Mates across the finish line. They'll now move on to face their next challenge, while Miami Heretics' Major IV run comes to an end in Paris.

OpTic Texas def. G2 Minnesota 3-0

OpTic Texas are through to the next round of CDL Major IV after completing a convincing 3-0 sweep over G2 Minnesota. After taking Scar Hardpoint and Gridlock Search and Destroy, OpTic closed the series on Den Overload to book their place in the winners bracket.

G2 looked poised to extend the series after striking first on Den Overload, taking an early 1-0 lead. OpTic responded immediately to level the score before taking control of the objective, moving ahead 2-1 as the momentum firmly shifted in their favor.

Dashy and Mercules took over the closing stages of the map. Dashy finished with a server-leading 32 kills and 3,628 damage, while Mercules added 29 kills, 3,845 damage, and three carrier kills to spearhead OpTic's offense. Their firepower proved too much for G2 to handle as OpTic secured the final point to win Den Overload 3-1 and complete the sweep.

The victory sends OpTic Texas into the Winners Final, where they'll face the winner of Los Angeles Thieves and FaZe Vegas. G2 Minnesota, meanwhile, drop into the elimination bracket and will take on Paris Gentle Mates for a chance to keep their Major IV campaign alive.

Los Angeles Thieves lead FaZe Vegas 2-1

FaZe Vegas have kept the series alive with a dominant 5-0 victory on Scar Overload, denying Los Angeles Thieves the sweep and cutting the deficit to 2-1.

After dropping the opening Hardpoint in dramatic fashion and falling 6-3 in Search and Destroy, FaZe responded with their best map of the series. They controlled Scar Overload from the opening engagement, never allowing Los Angeles to establish any momentum before completing a flawless 5-0 shutout.

Drazah led the charge with a server-best 39 kills, 4,429 damage, and six carrier kills, while Abuzah was equally influential with 32 kills, four overload captures, and 3,610 damage. Together, they spearheaded a complete team performance that left the Thieves searching for answers throughout the map.

Despite the setback, Los Angeles Thieves still hold a 2-1 series advantage and will have another opportunity to close out the match on Scar Hardpoint. FaZe Vegas, meanwhile, have finally found some momentum and will be hoping this emphatic victory sparks a reverse sweep.

FaZe Vegas have breathed new life into the final winners bracket series of Day 2, responding with a commanding 5-0 victory on Scar Overload to cut Los Angeles Thieves' lead to 2-1. The action now shifts back to Hardpoint, where the Thieves will look to close out the series while FaZe chase an unlikely reverse sweep.
