Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Respawnerz ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of this website. By accessing or using Respawnerz, you agree to the practices described below.

Respawnerz is a read-focused editorial website. We do not require you to create an account, register, or log in to access any content on this site.

The only personal information we collect is what you voluntarily provide when you contact us through our contact form. This includes your name, email address, and the contents of your message. We may also collect basic, non-identifiable usage data through standard server logs or analytics tools. Data Retention

We do not maintain a database of visitor information. Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to the inquiry, after which they are deleted.

Children's Privacy

Respawnerz is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under that age. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through this site, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.
